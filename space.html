Space

Space phenomena

  • Nebulae

    • Clouds of gas and dust in outer space, visible in the night sky either as an indistinct bright patch or as a dark silhouette against other luminous matter.


  • Supernovas

    • Stars that suddenly increases greatly in brightness because of a catastrophic explosion that ejects most of its mass.


  • Black holes

    • Regions of space having a gravitational field so intense that no matter or radiation can escape.
